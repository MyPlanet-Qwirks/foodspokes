What is FoodSpokes?

FoodSpokes is a consultancy and startup addressing food systems challenges founded by Vanessa Ling Yu. Foodspokes aims to create collaborative network initiatives in community, foodservice, and hospitality sectors.

The mission of foodspokes is to streamline food for better farm and restaurant businesses in Ontario. We want to make it easier for businesses in the food industry to communicate with one another.


About the Founder

Vanessa brings expertise in the areas of health and hospitality as Founder of FoodSpokes. Her experiences in creating meaningful engagement and integrating complex and diverse ideas inform her participatory approaches to building collaborative stakeholder initiatives.

Vanessa holds graduate degrees from the University of Toronto and University of Chicago and worked as a consultant for a number of local, provincial, and international organizations.
